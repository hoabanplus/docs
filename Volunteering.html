VOLUNTEERING
at HOA BAN +

Hoa Ban + are always looking for new volunteers either from Vietnam or abroad for short or long term projects. Volunteers are assisted by staff on site who can speak English and Vietnamese.

Volunteering at Hoa Ban+ can involve a variety of different activities:

  • Customer service in the store;
  • Promoting and selling the factory products on-line;
  • Developing new product design (bags, clothes, toys and accessories);
  • Teaching English language to the factory staff;
  • Support local community in the village.
1 day is 15 $ per person. Accommodation, 3 meals a day, water and fresh fruits are included. Volunteers are assisted by staff on site who can speak English and Vietnamese. Translation to other language service is not included, but we can help to find a translator.
We accept groups up to 50 people. Single volunteers are welcome as well. 
Volunteers can stay at least for 1 week. Singles and small groups can stay up to 6 month. 
We are open for volunteers all year round.
Please, take a note that in June - August the weather can be very hot and rainy.
